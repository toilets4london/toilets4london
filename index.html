5 star app for all your toilet-finding needs

Our free Android and iOS apps enable you to find toilets near you, view up-to-date detailed information, suggest changes to the data which get reviewed in under 24hrs, submit toilet ratings and suggest new toilet locations. You can also access our data using the London toilets web app. Our apps never have ads and never collect any user data or track you.

We make it easy to share toilet data

We write custom web scraping scripts that extract toilet data directly from reliable sources. As a organisation that manages toilets, nothing changes for you, while Toilets4London works behind the scenes to turn your data into a user-friendly useful mobile app. Get in touch with us today if you'd like us to pull data from your website, or any other source (google sheets/maps, excel etc.)! We also provide community toilet scheme managers with metrics on toilet ratings and reports submitted by app users.

Accurate info on the things that matter

Toilets4London collects data on accessibility, opening hours, baby change facilities and more so that you know exactly what is available. We regularly re-scrape and refresh the data, relying on direct links with facility providers, which means more up-to-date and accurate info for our users!

Join the Toilets4London Toilet Scheme

If you are a local business, opening up your toilet to the public is the best way to show that you care about improving the local area and be perceived as a brand that helps when it’s most needed. Businesses can join the Toilets4London toilet scheme and appear on our app today.

Three in four of the UK public think there are not enough public toilets in their area. Knowledge of lack of facilities nearby acts as a ‘loo leash’, deterring as many as one in five (20%) from venturing out of their homes as often as they would like. This rises to over two in five (43%) among those with medical conditions requiring frequent toilet use.
2018 Survey - Royal Society For Public Health
