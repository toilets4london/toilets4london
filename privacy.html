Privacy Policy

Mobile apps

The Toilets4London iOS and Android apps do not collect any personal user data. Any reviews or reports posted are not associated with any of your personal data and cannot be traced back to you. However, if you would like to cancel any of your submissions - either toilet ratings or reports - please contact nina@toilets4london.com and this will be done for you in under 24 hours. If you have any further questions about privacy or how we manage data, feel free to email us any questions.

Website
